Reading is a highly individual experience. The way text is presented can influence how comfortable it feels to read for extended periods. Some readers prefer wider spacing between lines, while others find certain fonts easier to follow. Background colour and contrast can also affect visual comfort. Use the controls above to experiment with different settings and discover what works best for you.
